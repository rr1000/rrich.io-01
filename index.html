About

I'm the Chief Product Officer at Datica where I help organizations large and small protect healthcare information in the cloud. At Datica I'm responsible for product design, strategy, and management.

Before Datica & Catalyze I worked with brands like Volkswagen and Ducati on automating their digital marketing strategies.
