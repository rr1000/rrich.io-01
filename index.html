about

My name is Ryan Rich. I’m a product and technology leader focused on building meaningful products and high-powered engineering teams.

I have a particular focus on privacy, security and regulatory compliance.

I’m currently working on a newly funded venture called Haekka. Before that, I was the Chief Product Officer at Datica where I helped organizations protect healthcare information in the cloud.

Before Datica, I co-founded a startup that helped educators streamline their grading process with the assistance of their mobile device. Utilizing optical character recognition technology (way before things like machine learning and AI were cool), I helped college professors drastically improve their grading process, allowing them to spend more time educating.

I live in Portland Oregon with my wife, daughter, dog and two cats.
